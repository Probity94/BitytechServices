About Us

Bitytech Services is geared towards helping P&O students and the Public to know more about Prosthetic and Orthotic Services. We hope to help P&O students who know little or nothing about the profession by providing online lectures and other materials needed. We hope to create awareness using this medium, to educate and bridge the gap between the public and P&O professionals, and also ensure that the needs of people living with Disabilities can be met. Especially in Africa.

Bitytech Services educates stidents about issues relating to Prosthetics and Orthotics, proffer solutions through personal experiences and research. Beneficial to all in need of Prosthetic and Orthotic Services.

What We Do

We hope to inform Africans living with different types of deformities that Prosthetic and Orthotic Services are available in Africa.
